How It Works

Understanding the mask detection technology

Detection Technology

MaskGuard uses advanced YOLO (You Only Look Once) AI model to detect and classify mask usage in real-time. The system analyzes video frames or images to identify faces and determine mask compliance status.

YOLO AI Model

State-of-the-art object detection for real-time processing

High Performance

Processes up to 10 frames per second with minimal latency

Privacy First

All processing happens locally - no data is stored or transmitted

Detection Categories

Mask Worn Correctly

Face mask properly covering nose and mouth

Green - Safe

Mask Worn Incorrectly

Mask not covering nose or worn improperly

Yellow - Warning

No Mask Detected

Person not wearing any face mask

Red - Alert

How to Use

1

Choose Detection Mode

Select between live camera detection for real-time monitoring or upload an image for analysis.

2

Grant Camera Access

For live detection, allow camera permissions when prompted. The system works entirely in your browser.

3

Monitor Detections

Watch as the system identifies faces and classifies mask usage with color-coded bounding boxes.

4

Respond to Alerts

Audio alerts notify you of violations. If more than 2 people are non-compliant, you'll hear "Environment Not Safe".

5

Track Statistics

View real-time compliance metrics and detection history. Reset statistics anytime with the reset button.

Alert System

Audio Alerts

Beep sound plays when a new violation is detected

Visual Warnings

Red bounding boxes and alert icons highlight violations

Voice Announcements

Speech synthesis announces "Environment Not Safe" when multiple violations occur

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my camera data stored or transmitted?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser. No images or video are stored or sent to any server.

What browsers are supported?

MaskGuard works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge that support WebRTC.

Can I use this on mobile devices?

Yes! The system is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets with camera access.

How accurate is the detection?

The YOLO model has been trained on thousands of images and achieves high accuracy in various lighting conditions.
